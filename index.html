GRC Specialist

Securing What Matters, Governing How It's Done

I am a results-driven IT and Cybersecurity Auditor with deep expertise in Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC). My work sits at the intersection of technology, law, and risk management — helping enterprises stay secure and audit-ready.

From conducting in-depth risk assessments and gap analyses to leading compliance reviews against frameworks such as ISO 27001, NIST CSF, SOC 2, and GDPR, I bring structured rigour to every engagement. I translate complex security findings into actionable board-level insights.

My approach is built on three pillars: identify the real risks, quantify their business impact, and remediate with proportionate, sustainable controls.

02 / Skills & Expertise

Where I Add Value

Cybersecurity Auditing

End-to-end IT and cybersecurity audits across enterprise environments — from scoping and evidence collection to finding validation and executive reporting.

Risk Assessment

Qualitative and quantitative risk assessments using NIST, FAIR, and ISO methodologies — identifying threats, vulnerabilities, and control gaps with business context.

Compliance & Regulatory

Driving compliance against ISO 27001, SOC 2, PCI-DSS, GDPR, and NDPR — including readiness assessments, remediation roadmaps, and certification support.

GRC Programme Design

Building and maturing GRC programmes from the ground up — policy development, control frameworks, risk registers, and continuous monitoring processes.

Vulnerability Management

Coordinating vulnerability scanning, penetration testing oversight, and remediation tracking to reduce organisational attack surface systematically.

Stakeholder Reporting

Translating technical audit findings and risk metrics into clear, actionable board reports, executive dashboards, and management presentations.
